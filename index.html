Hi, I am Alexandro Cazares, an aspiring software developer and a professional musician. Also a proud Hoosier of immigrant parents.

As a musician, I teach young musicians to be the best version of themselves on and off the stage. Also, I perform on the double bass in the classical and jazz community. But I feel like my musical passion is limited to only a certain group.

So I asked myself:

Self, what can I design and build that can reach millions of people and make a difference in the world?

Me in 2020
